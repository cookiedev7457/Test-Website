Staff Promotion Guidelines

How to Get Promoted

  • Activity: Consistent attendance at shifts and the store.
  • Professionalism: Use of grammar and mature behavior at all times.
  • Points/Experience: Reaching specific in-game milestones (e.g., 500 points for Junior Cashier).

Promotion Rules

Do not "hint" or ask for a promotion; this may result in a strike or blacklisting from future advancements.
